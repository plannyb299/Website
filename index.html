Welcome to FlighTechnologies

About us

FlighTech is a tech start-up company that mainly focuses on producing tailor made Unmanned Aerial Vehicle(UAV) technologies that help in improving the Health service delivery of Zimbabwe. After producing these vehicles, FlighTech then provides service delivery of medical essentials to different parts of Zimbabwe that are either far from the main centre or are hard to access due to bad road infrastructure or fuel shortages. FlighTech aims to provide state of the art technologies at low costs as compared to the already existing transportation methods which are being used to transport these medical essentials. We look forward to work hand in hand with the Ministry of Health of Zimbabwe and the Aviation Authority of Zimbabwe in striving for the betterment of health service delivery all around Zimbabwe.
